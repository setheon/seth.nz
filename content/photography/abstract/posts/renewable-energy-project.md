# Renewable Energy Project. 
### Phase 1; Wind Turbines (Ongoing Project)

In 2023 I started a project for a University
course focusing on photographing renewable energy infrastructure for a Zine, after my zine was complete I had already fallen in love with taking photos of renewable energy infrastructure and vowed to keep taking photos of these marvels of technology. Over years I'll keep adding to this project.
